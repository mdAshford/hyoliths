Supplementary Information for: Hyoliths with pedicles constrain the origin of the brachiopod body plan

Supplementary Information for: Hyoliths with pedicles constrain the origin of the brachiopod body plan

Haijing Sun, Martin R. Smith, Han Zeng, Fangchen Zhao, Guoxiang Li and Maoyan Zhu

2018-06-06

Supplementary Text

This document comtains supplementary material to H.-J. Sun et al. (2018).

It opens with a detailed discussion of analyses of the morphological dataset constructed to accompany H.-J. Sun et al. (2018), and their results.

The results presented in the main paper employ the algorithm described by Brazeau, Guillerme, & Smith (2018) for correct handling of inapplicable data in a parsimony setting. This document depicts how each character is most parsimoniously reconstructed on an optimal tree.

For completeness, we also document the results of standard Fitch parsimony analysis, and the results of Bayesian analysis, neither of which treat inapplicable data in a logically consistent fashion.

Supplementary figures and tables appear after the text.
